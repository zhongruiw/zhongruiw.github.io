Zhongrui Wang

I am a PhD student at the Department of Mathematics, University of Wisconsin-Madison, supervised by Prof. Nan Chen. Previously, I work with Prof. Lili Lei at the School of Atmospheric Sciences, Nanjing University on ensemble-based data assimilation. My research interests lie in reduced-order modeling for complex systems, data assimilation, and machine learning applications.

I can be reached by zhongrui[dot]wang@wisc.edu.
